Outdoors at UVa Policies

Since Outdoors at UVa is a student-run organization, it is important for us to write down how we manage the club, so future generations of leaders will have guidance to help the club succeed.

Mission Statement

Our Mission Statement defines our purpose, values, and principles as an organization.

Outdoors at UVa is a group of students and community members united by their desire to enjoy outdoor activities with friends, while promoting safety and environmental preservation, educating others about nature, and developing leadership skills.
