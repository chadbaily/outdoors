Please text the officer leading of the gear room if you will be late. Officers remain at the gear room only for as long as people are checking in/out gear (usually about 20-25 minutes). You may also purchase ODC t-shirts, hoo rags, bucket hats, and more at the gear room!

BEFORE RETURNING GEAR REFER TO THE GEAR CLEANING DOC HERE. If you return damaged, dirty, or incomplete gear, you will jeopardize your ability to check out gear in the future. It is your responsibility, not the officers, to return your gear in the condition you received it.

If none of these times work for you, please email the officers a few days in advance to schedule a separate time to be let into the gear room.
