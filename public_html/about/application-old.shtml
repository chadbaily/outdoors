Outdoors at UVa Officer Application

APPLICATION DUE BY MONDAY, APRIL 9, 2007

We are currently looking to add officers for next year. Please fill out the following if you are interested in becoming an officer in Outdoors at UVa. This application is designed to give us a good idea of the skills and background that you would bring to the club, as well as your interest in becoming an officer in the club. Please fill it out to the best of your abilities and let the know if you have any questions.

All officers work together to improve the club and decide on club policies. There is generally a lot of e-mail that gets sent to the officers list, and as an officer you must be willing to read and respond to these e-mails. A few officers handle money, some lead more trips than others, and some deal with gear more than others. The roles are flexible, but we all put a lot of energy into the club. The single most important aspect that we look for in new officers is a passion for the Club.
